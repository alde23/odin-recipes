Kung Pao Chicken Recipe

Kung Pao Chicken is a classic Chinese stir-fry dish known for its bold flavors.

To make Kung Pao Chicken, start by marinating diced chicken in soy sauce, rice vinegar, and a touch of cornstarch. In a hot pan or wok, stir-fry the chicken until cooked through, then set it aside. In the same pan, saut√© garlic, ginger, and dried red chilies until fragrant, followed by bell peppers and onions for a few minutes. Next, add roasted peanuts and the chicken back to the pan. For the sauce, combine soy sauce, hoisin sauce, sugar, and a bit of sesame oil, then pour it over the chicken and vegetables, cooking everything together until coated in a glossy, spicy sauce. Serve hot with steamed rice for a flavorful, satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Bell Peppers

Steps:

  1. Marinate chicken
  2. Stir fry chicken in a wok pan
  3. Saute garlic, ginger etc.
  4. Add roasted peanuts and the chicken back to the pan
  5. Add sauces
