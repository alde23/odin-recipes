Lasagna Recipe

The lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made primarily out of dough, tomato sauce and bechamel sauce as well as ground meat.

This classic homemade lasagna recipe features layers of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and perfectly cooked pasta, all baked to perfection with a golden, bubbly mozzarella topping. Start by browning ground beef with onions and garlic, then simmer it with crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and Italian herbs for a deep, savory flavor. Meanwhile, prepare a smooth ricotta mixture by blending ricotta cheese with an egg, Parmesan, and a touch of nutmeg for extra depth. Assemble the lasagna by layering cooked lasagna noodles with the meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella, repeating the layers until the dish is full. Bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 45 minutes, letting it rest before slicing to allow the flavors to meld beautifully. Serve with a sprinkle of fresh basil and a side of garlic bread for a comforting and satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Bechamel Sauce
  • Dough

Steps:

  1. Preapare the Ragu sauce
  2. Preapare the Bechamel sauce
  3. Assemble the lasagna
  4. Cook the lasagna at 375 degrees for approximately 45 minutes
