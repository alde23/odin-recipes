Carbonara Recipe

Carbonara serving recommendation

Begin by cooking pasta until al dente. In a separate pan, saut√© halal-friendly turkey bacon or chicken slices until crispy. For the sauce, whisk together eggs, grated Parmesan, and a pinch of black pepper. Once the pasta is cooked, quickly toss it with the turkey bacon and egg mixture, ensuring the heat from the pasta gently cooks the eggs into a velvety sauce. Add a bit of pasta water to achieve a silky consistency. Serve with extra Parmesan and a sprinkle of black pepper for a rich, comforting meal.

Ingredients:

  • Turkey Bacon
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan
  • Pasta
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Cook pasta
  2. Saute turkey bacon
  3. Whisk together eggs, salt, pepper and Parmesan.
  4. Mix sauce with pasta
